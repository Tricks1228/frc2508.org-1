Welcome to Armada Robotics!

FRC Team 2508, Armada Robotics, is an award winning FIRST Robotics team that operates out of Stillwater Area High School in Stillwater, Minnesota. Armada strives to challenge the youth of its community in a constructive manner through innovative development and teamwork.
